About Us

We are the Team Bleepy! We are 4th year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science students from City College of Calamba and this system is our thesis project served as partial fulfillment of the program that we are taking. This web application is called "Bleepy" as it is bleeping the profanities in the recorded videos. we are happy to present to you our developed system.

Our Vision

We envision that Bleepy will help to create a peaceful and enjoyable environment in the world of gaming

Our Mission

Our mission is to support the local streamers here in the Philippines to have a better content in their channels and eliminate the profanities that restricted by the community guidelines provided by the social media platforms in which game streamers upload their video
